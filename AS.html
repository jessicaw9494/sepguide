Academic Success

Tips

  • Arrive at school early
  • Study before and after class

    • Extra Credit

  • Impress your teachers by showing them work you do at home
  • When extra credit work is given, just do it!

    • Talk to Teachers

    Your teachers will get to know you better and eventually write you a sophisticated recommendation. (PS: This may also help your grade!!)


    Go To Tutoring!

    That 43 minutes of liberal help can do wonders!! In fact, working in school helps you stay more focused verses doing homework at home. Take advantage of this opportunity and get help! (Peer tutoring is also available~)


    Participate

  • Force yourself to speak in front of others. Not only will you get a better grade, but this will help your speech.
  • Go join clubs and try out for sports! Although this may be time consuming, you'll be able to find out what you really love and meet new friends.
