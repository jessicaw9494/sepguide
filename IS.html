Field Trips

In SEP, you will have a lot of opportunity to go to Field Trips and to different Tech Companies. The following are some tips for you to be successful during field trip.

  1. Be prepare and draft 2-5 questions(Don't ask question that you can find in Google)
  2. Challenge yourself to ask all of the questions
  3. Ask for opportunity such as internship if you know software is what you want to persue in the future.
